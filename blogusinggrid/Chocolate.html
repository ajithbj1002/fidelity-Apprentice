Decadent Chocolate Cake

Indulge in our rich, moist chocolate cake recipe that's perfect for any occasion. This heavenly dessert is a chocolate lover's dream come true, combining deep cocoa flavors with a light, fluffy texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Mix well.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla to the dry ingredients. Mix until well blended.
  4. Stir in the hot coffee. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  8. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!
