Zesty Lemon Tart

Experience the perfect balance of sweet and tart with our classic lemon tart recipe. A buttery crust filled with bright, citrusy goodness makes this dessert a refreshing treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg yolk

For the filling:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • Zest of 2 lemons

Instructions

  1. For the crust, pulse flour, sugar, and salt in a food processor. Add butter and pulse until mixture resembles coarse meal.
  2. Add egg yolk and pulse until dough comes together. Press into a 9-inch tart pan. Freeze for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake crust for 20 minutes until golden. Cool completely.
  4. For the filling, whisk together eggs, sugar, lemon juice, cream, and zest in a bowl.
  5. Pour filling into cooled crust. Bake for 15-20 minutes until filling is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  6. Cool completely, then chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
  7. Dust with powdered sugar and garnish with lemon slices if desired.
