Sweet Strawberry Cupcakes

Delight in our sweet strawberry cupcakes topped with creamy strawberry frosting. These little bites of happiness are perfect for spring gatherings, bringing a burst of fruity flavor to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup finely chopped fresh strawberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Gradually add in the flour mixture alternating with milk, mixing until just incorporated.
  6. Gently fold in chopped strawberries.
  7. Fill cupcake liners about 2/3 full with batter.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Once cooled, frost with strawberry buttercream and garnish with a fresh strawberry slice.
